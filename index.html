How it works

  1. IThink of a four or five letter word. Commit to it.
  2. IIThink of one of the playing cards on the wheel opposite.
  3. IIIYou can rotate the disc of playing cards to view them.
  4. IVWhen you are ready, press Begin at the centre of the wheel. Rotate the disc so your card is against the first letter of your word. You can drag from anywhere on the inner ring — that way I cannot know which card you are thinking of.
    Press Confirm at the centre of the wheel when done.
  5. VDo this for each letter in your word.
    When done, sit back and think of your word. I'm going to read your mind.

Robert Wesley Hummer

1906–1981
